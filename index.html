About Me

Mapping Stories from Above

Geomatics Engineer with a Passion for Earth Observation

Hi! I'm Yuliana, a Geomatics Engineering student at UPN "Veteran" Yogyakarta with a strong interest in remote sensing, GIS, and environmental monitoring. I enjoy exploring how Earth observation data can be used to analyze various environmental phenomena and spatial patterns across land, water, and atmospheric domains.

Through academic projects and NASA ARSET trainings, I have developed hands-on experience in satellite data processing, spatial analysis, and GIS-based visualization. I am actively involved in student organizations, where I contribute to research programs, drone-based mapping, and technical training activities.
